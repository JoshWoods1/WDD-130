Content

Home page

Storm's Drive-In has been around since 1959. Located in the heart of Lampasas County, it stands ready to serve every person who comes through in a timely manner. When you come, come ready to have the best food that you've ever had, and bring your friends too! Come ready to eat anything from our famous Storm's Special to our delicious shakes!

Images for the Home page

Burgers and Sides

Come try our Storm's Special which comes with a side of french fries, or swap em' out with our amazing spicy curly fries or whatever you'd like. And for the little ones, we have our own mini burger that we guarantee they'll love, dressed however way you'd like. If you're not feeling a burger come try one of our dinners with choices between chicken, steak, catfish or shrimp.

Images for the Page 2

Drinks & Shakes

Wash down your food with some of the best Dr. Pepper in town! With a choice of any of our coke products, or a famous cherry lime squeeze, or our freshly brewed iced tea, we guarantee you'll love our drinks just as much as our food. And for dessert you can try one our amazing shakes with a choice of 5 different flavors, or come and get some of your favorite soft served ice cream.
